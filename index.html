Who is SA-20 Productions, LLC

Jacki Lippman and Liz Brock Lewis have been friends for decades. They are once again working together producing quality programming that is engaging, entertaining, meaningful and that celebrates the human spirit.
Their programs will be marketable for theatrical distribution as well as for streaming, cable and broadcast.

About Jacki Lippman

Jacki is an independent film producer with three feature films currently available on Amazon Prime (Stakeout, Chariot and Nandor Fodor) with a fourth coming out in August 2025, (Hellfire), and a fifth in development.
Prior to her work in the film industry, Jacki was a professional media and events coordinator for Grameen Foundation, the US resource center for Grameen Trust based in Washington DC, and Grameen America, a Grameen operated U.S. replication, based in New York.
She produced media events in Boston, Washington, DC, New York City and Los Angeles.

About Liz Brock Lewis

As a Washington, D.C. native, a career working for the U.S. Government was a natural step for Liz after graduating from University.
She spent half a dozen years working for Uncle Sam with three of those serving as a U.S. Diplomat assigned to the U.S. Consulate in Milan, Italy. Upon leaving the Foreign Service, Liz pursued a career in law and spent over twenty years working in Litigation. As an over the top dog-mom to several rescue pups over the years, she took her skills and worked in the non-profit sector advocating on behalf of animal welfare issues and organizations. A passion which she continues still wherever she can.
Liz has spent the last couple of years living in SE Asia and traveling the region in search of and certainly finding interesting characters and stories she intends to put to paper. She is currently working on a script about a young English lad in the UK with an unconventional career choice who finds himself involved in a murder.
